A narrative is a route through entries for a reader.

Entries are addressable but unordered.
A reader who meets them in arbitrary order gets facts without understanding.
A narrative supplies an intentional route that makes one idea lead to the next.

A narrative sequences,
motivates,
and connects.
It should explain why the route matters,
keep the sequence tight,
and point to the entries that carry durable detail instead of restating them.

The core lake includes `narrative` as the default category for readable routes
through a project's ideas.
