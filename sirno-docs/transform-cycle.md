The *transform* cycle is the review front door for the four movements between Sirno *forms*.

`lower`, `realize`, `reflect`, and `raise` are not independent.
They form one loop: narrative becomes *lake entries*, *entries* guide the *repository*,
the *repository* feeds durable facts back, and the *lake* can compose into a *monograph*.
A change to one movement usually changes what the others must preserve.

This *entry* gives that loop a horizontal review neighborhood.
A reviewer who touches one movement should visit the others through this front door
to confirm the round trip still holds.

`transform` remains the broader concept each movement `refines`.
This neighborhood is the separate horizontal view:
`refines` says what a movement specializes,
`belongs` here says which movements are reviewed together.
